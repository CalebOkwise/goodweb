Course Overview

Ethical Hacking Certification (CEH v12) Training Course is one of the globally-recognized cybersecurity courses for professionals globally. This CEH Certification course is ideal for professionals who manage IT security aspect across infrastructure that overarches physical, cloud, and hybrid environments.

Duration: 14 weeks / Beginner level Tuition: Starting from #100,000
