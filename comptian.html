Course Overview

The CompTIA Network course builds on your existing user-level knowledge and experience with personal computer operating systems and networks to present fundamental skills and concepts that you will use on the job in any type of networking career. If you are pursuing a CompTIA technical certification path, the CompTIA A certification is an excellent first step to take before preparing for the CompTIA Network certification. You will identify and describe all the major networking technologies, systems, skills, and tools in use in modern PC-based computer networks, and learn information and skills that will be helpful as you prepare for the CompTIA Network certification examination.

Duration: 8 weeks / Beginner level Tuition: Starting from #70,000
