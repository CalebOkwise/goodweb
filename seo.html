Course Overview

This course explains how to optimise website for search engines and what techniques are good to bring a website on first page of Google. In this course we will discuss and practically implement both on-site and off-site SEO techniques. A practical Search Engine Optimisation project at the end of course will ensure that you go through complete SEO process by researching, planning, utilising tools and techniques.

This short SEO course is hands-on, instructor-led and classroom based. This training can be taken as a part-time evening course or even on the weekends.

Duration: 16 weeks / Beginner level Tuition: Starting from #120,000
